Wanna get High?

The objective of the game is to get high with care. Without exaggerating the dose or making dangerous combinations.

Check what is in your pocket. This is what you can use. If your pocket gets empty, you loose one life.

Once you get to the top you win and can go to the next level.

Use the keyboards keys CAMT HELP and arrows to play
